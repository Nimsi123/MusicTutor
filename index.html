Welcome to MusicTutor, Decathletes!



As a former Academic Decathlon competitor, I thought students like myself could benefit from mechanical practice with basic music theory questions. MusicTutor randomly generates a practically unlimited number of practice questions. I developed MusicTutor at first for my team, and I later developed this interactive website for students across the country. I hope you'll find it useful!
